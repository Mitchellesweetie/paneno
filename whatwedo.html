About the Firm

Paneno Crime Security and Safety Watch Company Ltd is a Regional training and con- sulting firm whose clarion call is to “capacity build and also provide research based security and safety solutions” to our clients. Our target client networks include private and government sector institutions, churches, hotels, banks and financial institutions, Security Companies, Non-governmental Organizations, and Research institutions in East and Central Africa. The company was founded by two flamboyant, polished, passion- ate, pragmatic and experienced security and safety experts with the zeal to empower clients at personal and institutional levels and to raise their security and safety stan- dards and target harden them from both internal and external security threats in this epoch of rising insecurity.

Each trainer has over fifteen years practical work experience and training. We believe strongly that training combined with the genuine desire to apply bench marked best practices will improve security and safety solutions substantially; at both personal securi- ty and organizational security. We focus on giving people in private and public sectors the opportunity to sharpen, upgrade their skills and improve their performance in order to build positive, steady, strong institutions and create value on a sustainable basis.

Paneno Crime Security and Safety Watch Company Limited is positioned to offer the highest standards of Training and Consultancy, organize interactive, practical, applica- ble and value-adding open and in-house training courses, seminars and workshops for all cadres of staff. Our events cover a wide range of topics for: Training in Disaster and incidents management, Fire safety, Fraud detection and prevention, Personal security, Criminal and Forensic investigations, Security Threats and Risk Assessments, Security Risk Management, Training of security guards amongst others.
